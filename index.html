About Us

FarSide Data & Security is a locally owned and operated business located in Kyabram, we specialize in providing security solutions to commercial and residential premises throughout Victoria.

We provide installations, maintenance and 24/7 monitoring for new and existing alarm and CCTV systems designed specifically for each customer, as well as wireless and hardwired data network installations and testing.

FarSide Data & Security now also offer repairs and servicing for small electronics such as mobile phones, laptops and computers.

Our Services

CCTV & Alarm Systems

FarSide Data & Security provide electronic security installations and maintenance to all types of new and existing CCTV and alarm systems, including upgrading exisiting systems, changing login codes/PINs and system tests and more.

Emergency GPS Pendants

We offer NEVERalone Emergency GPS Pendants designed for the elderly and also lone workers. These pendants are monitored 24/7 to ensure the user is safe at all time, for more information check the Gallery Page or contact us any time below.

Data Networks

We maintain data network cabling with high quality CAT6 network cable, as well as wireless network links between buildings and network fault finding and testing to ensure your data network works smoothly every time.

Alarm Monitoring

FarSide offer 24/7 alarm monitoring for alarm systems and duress pendants through the 3G/4G network allowing up to date information regarding your system, this includes faults such as low battery warnings, power fail, communications loss, etc.

Electronics Servicing & Repairs

We provide servicing and repairs for electronics such as mobile phones, computers, laptops etc.
