About Us

FarSide Data & Security is a locally owned and operated business located in Kyabram, we provide security solutions, electronics repairs and IT solutions to commercial and residential premises throughout Victoria.

We provide installations, maintenance and 24/7 monitoring for new and existing alarm and CCTV systems designed specifically for each customer, as well as wireless and hardwired data network installations and testing.

We also offer repairs, upgrades and servicing to all types of electronics such as mobile phones, tablets and computers.
Other Repairs and IT Services also available, please contact us for more details.
