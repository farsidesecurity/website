About Us

FarSide Data & Security is a locally owned and operated business located in Kyabram providing security solutions to commercial and residential premises throughout Victoria.

We provide installations, maintenance and 24/7 monitoring for new and existing alarm and CCTV systems designed specifically for each customer, depending on their wants and needs as well as wireless and hardwired data network installations and testing.

FarSide Data & Security now also provides small electronic repairs and servicing for brown goods such as mobile phones, laptops and computers.
