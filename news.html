NEW Emergency GPS Pendants Now Available!

FarSide Data & Security are now offering wireless emergency pendants, suitable for people in all situations, from the elderly to people in high risk work places or even farmers. A user can press the emergency button on the device if under duress or in need of assistance.

An emergency message together with your location will be sent to our monitoring station, the control room will then talk to you with the two way hands-free voice communication within the pendant to ensure you're okay and to take any precautions necessary. It can also use the inbuilt shock detection to notify the monitoring station if you experience a sudden fall.
